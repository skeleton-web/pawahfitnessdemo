Physical Fitness

Gym Equipment

Our repertoires of equipment are the best of top grades, tested and proven to serve the needs for which they are procured. They have optimal life span and best warranty coverage you can ever get. Remarkably, not one of our brandish of clients has ever had need to invoke the warranty terms owing to the heightened reliability indices of our supplies.

Supplies

We are experienced procurers of gym wares and equipment. Our requisition covers all products within and outside the demand limits of our clientele. We frequently and optimally update our cache with the most recent and most efficient equipment available to the fitness and wellness industry. We offer two to seven years warranty on our equipment.

Training

Fitness is our business, customer's satisfaction is our priority. We are aware of the eminent frustrations that come with procuring equipment whose use is unknown to the buyer. We understand that equipment manuals can be boring and in some cases nauseating, we therefore mapped out a reliable solution that has helped us maintain customer loyalty over the years. PAWAHFITNESS offers the best in training in areas of equipment handling, routine checks, troubleshooting procedures and basic maintenance.

Maintenance

The life span of any equipment is greatly affected by handling. With poor handling and deviation from usage instructions depreciation sets in faster. We know these and therefore cannot trust any other hands to better maintain the class of equipment we supply. Our engineers are top notch and vast in their disciplines. Suffice to say, the wealth of experience we readily have at our disposal sees maintenance tasks delivered on schedule, to specification and to industry standards.

Consultancy

Apart from supplies, maintenance and training, we deploy a reliable knowledge base in offering excellent consultancy services in all spheres of physical fitness.
